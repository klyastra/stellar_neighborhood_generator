MAIN SCRIPT:
* stargen.py

Primary dependencies of stargen.py:
  > stargen_funcs (for generating stars, computing their properties, plotting & exporting)
  > star_class (where the Star class/object is defined.
                Derived attributes are computed using secondary dependencies.)


stargen_funcs and star_class uses secondary modules. These include:
* spectral_types --> star_class
* stefan_boltzmann_funcs --> MLT_relations --> star_class
